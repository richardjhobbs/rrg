Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 March 2026
01

What We Collect

When you purchase a product on RRG, we collect:

  • Your wallet address (public, on-chain)
  • Transaction details (payment amount, token ID, timestamp)
  • Email address, if you choose to provide one

We do not require an account or personal profile to browse or purchase.

02

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

  • Process and deliver your digital purchase
  • Provide access to downloadable files attached to your product
  • Communicate about your order if needed
  • Improve the platform

We do not sell your data to third parties.

03

On-Chain Data

Your wallet address and purchase transactions are recorded on the Base blockchain. This data is public and permanent by design — it is not controlled by RRG and cannot be deleted.

04

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for essential site functionality (authentication sessions, preferences). We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

05

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for payment processing, file storage, and hosting. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and process your data only as needed to deliver our service.

06

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

07

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data (excluding on-chain records) by contacting us. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

08

Children

RRG is not directed at anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

09

Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version is always available at this page.

10

Contact
